Hi, I'm Filip Jankovic

I'm a Data Scientist looking for challenging problems to solve using my skills in statistical analysis, visualization, and machine learning.
Scroll down to learn more.

About Me

I am a recent graduate from the University of Michigan’s Industrial and Operations Engineering Master’s program. This past year, I have been working on the Applied Data Science with Python Specialization on Coursera.org.

I primarily work in Python with extensive knowledge of packages such as Numpy, Pandas and Scikit-Learn but also have experience with R and Matlab.

Outside of the realm of data science, I also enjoy travelling, wilderness backpacking, running, and exploring new food.

If you would like to get in touch, please email me at filipj@umich.edu.
