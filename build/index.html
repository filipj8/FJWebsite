About Me

I am a recent graduate from the University of Michigan’s Industrial and Operations Engineering Master’s program. This past year, I have been working as a lead content developer on the Applied Data Science with Python Specialization on Coursera.org.

I primarily work in Python with extensive knowledge of packages such as Numpy, Pandas and Scikit-Learn and also have experience with R and Matlab.

Outside of the realm of data science, I enjoy traveling, wilderness backpacking, running, and playing trivia.

If you would like to get in touch, please email me at filipj@umich.edu.
